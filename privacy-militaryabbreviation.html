Privacy Protocol

Application: Military Abbreviation

Developer: ZyeemZia™

Effective Date: June 2026

This privacy protocol establishes the data handling practices for the Military Abbreviation application ("App"). Your privacy and operational security are prioritized. This document details what information is collected, how it is processed, and the third-party services integrated within the framework.

1. Information Collection & Telemetry

The App itself does not passively collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information (PII) to external developer servers. Core translation matrix and abbreviation lookups operate locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Advertising Networks

To support ongoing development, the App integrates secure advertising frameworks provided by Google AdMob and the Meta Audience Network. These third-party networks may collect specific telemetry to serve relevant operational advertisements:

  • Device Identifiers: Advertising IDs (such as Google Advertising ID) for ad targeting and frequency capping.
  • Usage Data: Interaction metrics with served advertisements (clicks, views).
  • Network Information: IP addresses and general location data derived from the network to ensure regional ad compliance.

These networks operate under their respective privacy policies and strict data governance protocols.

3. Data Security & Retention

All temporary data processed for advertising rendering utilizes standard encryption protocols provided by the respective SDKs (Software Development Kits). The developer does not retain or possess access to raw user advertising profiles.

4. User Controls & Opt-Out Protocols

You maintain control over your advertising telemetry. You can reset your device's Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ad tracking directly through your Android device's system settings (Settings > Google > Ads).

5. Children's Privacy

This tactical utility is not directed toward individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal telemetry from children.

6. Protocol Revisions

This matrix may be updated to reflect changes in software architecture or legal requirements. Continued utilization of the App following updates signifies acknowledgment of the revised protocol.

7. Signal Support Contact

For inquiries regarding this privacy protocol or data handling mechanisms, contact the developer at: zyeem@zyeemzia.com.
